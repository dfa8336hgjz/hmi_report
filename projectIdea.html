Project Idea

Initial Idea

     In today's digital era, managing and accessing documents efficiently is essential for productivity and collaboration. With the rise of online platforms like Google Drive and Microsoft OneDrive, users can store and share a multitude of documents with ease. However, despite the usefulness of these platforms, finding specific information within documents can be a challenge due to inefficient search functions and less user-friendly navigation. To address these issues, we conceived the idea of developing a new document chatbot, built to enhance the way users interact with and explore their files. Our goal is to create an intuitive interface where documents are accessible through natural language queries, enabling users to quickly locate and retrieve the information they need with minimal effort.

Refined Idea

     After receiving feedback from our instructors, we made several important changes to our project. The feedback emphasized the need to improve user experience and make it easier for users to find and interact with their documents. We decided to focus on enhancing the search functionality by incorporating smart filters that allow users to locate documents based on categories such as file type, date modified, and content keywords. With these updates, we aim to create a document chatbot that not only provides quick access to files but also delivers a seamless and efficient user experience for managing and retrieving information.
